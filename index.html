How to Play

Unscramble the Rubik's cube so every face shows the target word grid.

The Goal

  • Each face of the cube has a 3x3 grid of letters
  • When solved, every row and column on every face spells a valid 3-letter word
  • All 6 faces must show the same word grid with matching colors

How to Move

  • Drag on a sticker to rotate that layer of the cube
  • Drag on empty space to orbit/rotate your view
  • Or use the button controls below the cube

Daily Puzzle

  • Monday = 1 move to solve (easy!)
  • Sunday = 7 moves to solve (hard!)
  • Everyone gets the same puzzle each day
  • Try to solve it in the fewest moves — par = the minimum!

Tips

  • Use Hint to see how many moves away you are
  • Use Undo if you make a wrong move
  • The colors tell you which face each sticker belongs to
  • Share your result to challenge friends!
